About Tripex Enterprises Limited

Tripex Enterprises Limited is a dynamic floor and carpet covering, building Construction and general supplies company started out over 6 years ago but continually striving to improve integrity, professionalism and service by empowering our staff, associates and clients with knowledge and expertise.

Our reputation for installation quality carpets, building Construction and general supplies in Kenya is built on a strict code of requirements. At Tripex, we are highly experienced, knowledgeable and dedicated to upholding professional standards in workmanship.

Our Products

We offer a diverse range of high-quality floor and carpet coverings, from luxurious shags and durable commercial carpets to elegant area rugs, all designed to enhance comfort and aesthetics in any space.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of innovative and sustainable floor and carpet covering, building construction, and general supply solutions in Kenya, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, integrity, and client satisfaction.

  • Interior Specialist

    Tripex Enterprises Limited offers a full range of interior design and interior architectural services which include planning, designing, project management, decorating services, deriving project costs, supervising, quality control, budget monitoring and coordination of the entire project.

  • General Supplies

    Tripex offer procurement, installation and maintenance services for all Interior requirements across all interior construction sectors.
